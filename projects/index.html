All Projects

Team Team Members Project Title Project Description
Team-1 Manisha Shrestha, Jhamka P. Gautam, Sajani Lama, Nishtha Rajbhandari Inclusive Education for the Disabled - Are We Doing Enough? An analysis on the impact of existing government provisions towards the goal of inclusive education for the disabled in Nepal.
Team-2 Pawan K Jha, Madhu Sudan Gautam, Ojeshwi Risal, Bhim Gautam Assessing the livelihood recovery of earthquake impacted households in most affected districts of Nepal We will identify the recovery dimensions and indicators of livelihood recovery based on secondary literature. Recovery will be assessed on the resilience framework. The secondary data for the project will be extracted from the HRRP (http://www.hrrpnepal.org/) project. This project will map out the private housing reconstruction and sectoral interventions in the most earthquake affected districts of Nepal. This will help us to make a narrative of assessment of recovery.
Team-3 Anjita Parajuli, Ankush Uprety, Chimi Seldon, Abyaya Neopane, Chandni Singh Impact of Gender and Ethnicity on Student's Learning Outcomes A case study of one of the local government based on 2075 Grade 8 examination
Team-4 Sarita Sharma, Soni Khanal, Sagar Ghimire, Sanhita Sahasrabudhe, Suluv Nepal Can economic development and happiness can be linked? Exploring the trends on GDP, Poverty and Happiness Index
Team-5 Anand Gautam, Jamuna Siwakoti, Nitee Shrestha, Ankit Raj Khatri, Nishant Khanal Policy report on Budget spending of Provinces in Nepal We want to compare the province level budget allocation of the 7 provinces in Nepal. We will look into two years provinces budget, compare that with the expenditure of the provinces.
Team-6 Pranita Upadhyaya, Rojina Shrestha, Sunil Kumar Jha, Tirupati Pariyar, Utsav Maden The role of female ownership on Education We try find out the correlation ship between female ownership on Education
Team-7 Debabrat Sukla, Bijay Timalsina, Utkrista Parajuli, Raunak Raj, Bhrikuti Rai How safe is bottled drinking water available in Kathmandu. We will try to explore whether or not bottled water producers are abiding with government rules.
Team-8 Bikin Ghimire, Abhishek Bhakta Shrestha, Sunita Basnet, Ojeshwini Singh Are taxi drivers properly being prosecuted, and is the medium of reports made affecting it? Analyze a data from the past 3 years on prosecution of taxi drivers according to dates, medium used to make the report, and compare them time wise to check if any policy reformations had an affect on it.
Team-9 Dipti Gautam, Samikshya Siwakoti, Sunanda Sharma, Ujwal Adhikari, Pushpa Raj Acharya Women's participation in political leadership Deriving insights from various elections data sets in Nepal as well as international researches to look at the issue of female candidacy and leadership in politics.
Team-10 Bikesh Gautam, Sunita Pariyar, Subash Pandey, Bal Krishna Khadka, Samanata Thapa Conditions of fertility decline in Nepal We will be looking at 5 different factors declining the fertility rate in Nepal.
Team-11 Bishnu Karki, Kalyan Adhikari, Shweta Acharya, Vikram Upadhyay, Sanjaya Pudasaini, Tsering D Gurung Access to Justice for Rape Victims in Nepal To conduct a situation analysis on rape incidents in Nepal, determine correlations with different causes and factors, and identify the gaps in our judicial response.
Team-12 Babita Basnet, Sanjeev Dhungel, Sunil Pokharel, Tilak Pathak, Srawan Kumar KC, Prakriti Dhungana How education impacts on HDI at provincial level of Nepal Linking education status with HDI
Team-13 Alina Chhantel, Gopal Nepali, Manish Garba, Pravin Dangol, Roshee Lamichhane Bhusal Female Labor Migrants of Nepal Trend Analysis using the gender perspective, consequence of female labor migration
Team-14 BP Sah, Swarup Acharya, Santona Devkota KC, Seana Lama Comparative Analysis of the school drop out rate and its cause in classes 8-10 in years 2016-2018. We will be analyzing the data of year 2016-18 regarding enrollment rate, passed out rate, drop out rate, investment, in relation with gender parity, student teacher ratio, number of community and private school.
Team-15 Sushmita Lama, Anish Bhurtel, Prajjwol Lakhey, Anupama Pant, Dipesh Karki Access to Finance for Women We are planning to study the women's access to finance in Nepal. And factors that affect them
Team-16 Nikesh Balami, Sarita Neupane, Siddhika Deoja, Sijal Pokharel, Umesh Rajopadhyaya Impact of Air Pollution in Kathmandu Valley Analyze the Air Pollution Data of Kathmandu Valley and compare the different indicators, to find its impact on the health of people living in the Kathmandu.
Team-17 Shyam Kumar Thapa, Pratikshya Sharma, Bikal Rai, Nisha Adhikari, Gaurav Kandel Impact of skill based training for ensuring decent work We will be working on skill gap where we will look into the data related with the labor working in the foreign country. Further we will be exploring the data showing the impact of having skill based training beforehand in order to get decent work.
