Title to explain the gist of the project/workshop


Data literacy can support Nepal government’s priorities by fostering an environment where citizens have the knowledge to use data for evidence-based decision making and to increase transparency and accountability in public service delivery. Data literacy can also potentially lead to innovative solutions that promote economic growth and achieve better living conditions of people. To this end, World Bank is organizing Data Literacy training for academics, CSOs and members of media, and hackathon for diverse group of stakeholders to:

  • increase their data analysis capacity for data driven decision making and
  • develop and pilot data-driven projects.
